###Cyber Security.

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting computers, networks, applications, and data from unauthorized access, cyber attacks, damage, or theft.

####It focuses on safeguarding:

- Personal data (passwords, bank details)

- Organizational data (customer records, intellectual property)

- Systems and networks (servers, cloud, websites)

####Goals of Cyber Security

- Prevent cyber attacks

- Protect sensitive information

- Ensure safe and reliable system operation

####Example:
Protecting an online banking system from hackers and fraud.

###CIA Triad (Foundation of Cyber Security)

The CIA Triad represents the three core principles of cyber security:

###1 Confidentiality.

Ensures that only authorized users can access information.

####How it is achieved

- Passwords & authentication

- Encryption

- Access control

####Example:
Only doctors can access patient medical records.

###2 Integrity.

Ensures that data remains accurate, complete, and unaltered.

####How it is achieved

- Hashing

- Digital signatures

- Checksums

####Example:
A bank transaction amount is not changed during transfer.

###3 Availability.

Ensures that systems and data are accessible when needed.

####How it is achieved

- Backups

- Redundancy

- DDoS protection

####Example:
A website remains available even during heavy traffic.
